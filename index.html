Welcome to my github pages, which I mostly use at this time to keep track of practice problems I am working through. Post Illa, the title of this site, is Latin for after those, and was used as a sort of standard intro for exigetical commentaries on texts common in 13th century european university centers. You can think of it as meaning roughly "margin notes", which is what I provide here on common coding challenges.
